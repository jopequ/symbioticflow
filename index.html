The Challenge

Railway hub logistics are stuck in the manual era

Every night, tens of thousands of parcels flow through railway hubs under extreme time pressure. The entire process — pulling trolleys from wagons, unloading parcels onto sorting belts — is done by hand.

⏱

Extreme Time Pressure

Operations run between 3:00 and 7:30 AM. Every minute counts to get parcels sorted and loaded for morning delivery.

⚙

Unstructured Environments

Railway hubs have rails, uneven floors, grates, and tight spaces. No two hubs look the same. Standard automation cannot cope.

📦

Heterogeneous Cargo

Parcels vary wildly in size, weight, shape, and fragility. No standardized loading patterns exist.

Our Approach

The right robot for the right task

Instead of one robot trying to do everything, we pair two specialized platforms — each excelling where the other can't — coordinated by an intelligent AI layer.

Dexterous Node

Wheeled Humanoid

Galaxea R1 Pro — Binabik AI
  • AI vision identifies parcel type, size, and orientation
  • Dual arms with 26 degrees of freedom
  • Picks heterogeneous parcels from trolleys onto belt
  • Adapts to novel objects without retraining
All-Terrain Tugger

Wheeled-Legged Quadruped

LYNX M20 Pro — DEEPRobotics
  • Tows trolleys between wagons and sorting stations
  • Hybrid locomotion: wheels for speed, legs for obstacles
  • Navigates rails, gaps, and uneven terrain
  • All-weather operation (IP66)
🧠

Binabik Agentic OS

Vision-language models reason about tasks in real time. The AI layer decomposes high-level goals, dispatches specialized skills to each robot, and adapts to unexpected situations — enabling a modular system that extends to new tasks across the logistics chain.

Concept of Operations

A continuous, synchronized loop

1

Retrieve

Quadruped navigates to wagon, engages hitch, secures trolley

2

Transport

Tows trolley across hub terrain to the sorting station

3

Unload

Humanoid uses AI vision and dual arms to pick parcels onto belt

4

Return

Quadruped swaps empty trolley for a full one — cycle repeats
